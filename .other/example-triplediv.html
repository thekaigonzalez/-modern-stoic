One Type of Website Layout


This website demonstrates a basic layout where there is one big divider, with three smaller ones to the left, right, and the middle.

This was quite easy to implement, you float both the right and left one to their respective directions, you set the middle one's margin to zero, and you simply place it after the declarations of the right and left ones, the middle one can not go in the middle of the declarations, it must be the last one.
